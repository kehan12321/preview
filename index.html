Archideators Studio was founded on a belief that architecture is more than construction — it is the act of shaping how people experience time, light, and belonging. Based in Pakistan, our practice spans residential, commercial, cultural, and urban scales.

We work closely with clients from the very first conversation through to handover, ensuring that every decision — structural, spatial, material — serves the singular vision we establish together.

Our team brings together architects, interior designers, urban planners, and visualisation specialists under one roof, allowing us to deliver a truly integrated service with no handoff gaps and no diluted vision.

03 — What We Offer

Design disciplines & specialisms

A full spectrum of architectural services — from concept to completion — delivered with precision and care at every stage.

A
01Architecture↗
Full architectural services from concept design through technical documentation, contractor management and site supervision to handover.
02Interior Design↗
Holistic interior environments — spatial planning, material palettes, lighting design, and FF&E procurement for residential and commercial clients.
03Urban & Master Planning↗
Strategic urban design and masterplanning for mixed-use developments, housing schemes, and civic spaces at neighbourhood and city scale.
04Landscape Design↗
Landscape and outdoor environments designed as extensions of architecture — gardens, courtyards, public realm, and sustainable green infrastructure.
053D Visualisation↗
Photorealistic rendering, animation, and virtual walkthroughs to communicate design intent clearly to clients, stakeholders, and planning authorities.
06Sustainable Design↗
Environmentally responsible architecture embedded from day one — passive strategies, local materials, solar analysis, and green building certification guidance.
